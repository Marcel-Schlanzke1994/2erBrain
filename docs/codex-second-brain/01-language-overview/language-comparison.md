|---|---|---|---|---|
| Learning curve | Low to medium | Medium to high | Low | High |
| Readability | High | Medium | High | Medium when disciplined |
| Execution speed | Medium | High | Medium | High |
| Memory control | Low | Very high | Low | High |
| Error risk | Medium | High | Medium | High without guidelines |
| Testability | High | Medium | High | Medium to high |
| Maintainability | High with typing/tests | Medium | High for small scripts | High with RAII and clear ownership |
| Portability | High | Very high | High | High with toolchain care |
| Embedded fit | Low | Excellent | Good when embedded | Good to excellent |
| Backend fit | Excellent | Poor | Limited | Good for high-performance services |
| AI fit | Excellent | Poor | Limited | Good for inference/runtime cores |

| Gameplay scripting | Possible | Poor | Excellent | Good for core rules |
| Unreal fit | Tooling | Not primary | Via plugins | Primary native language |
